HOLLOW KNIGHT: Silksong

Hollow Knight: Silksong is the sequal to Hollow Knight, released in 2025, it lets you play as one of the villains from the original game named Hornet.

Silksong is the best souls-like/metroidvania game created, my favorite aspect being the 200+ unique boss fights

Silksong boss fights

Boss fights in Silksong are different from Hollow Knight in many aspects. to beat a Silksong boss, requires you to
forget everything you thought you knew about the game mechanics and combat system, and re-learn how to play against
a given boss, Team Cherry really went all out to develope each of the 200+ bosses with this core idea.
